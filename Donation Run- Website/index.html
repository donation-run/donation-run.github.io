About Us

“No one has ever become poor by giving.” - Anne Frank

We made this game to encourage people to view donations from a different perspective. Our goal is for society to be more inclined to donate and give back to their community members that are in need of clothes, food, access to better healthcare and education, and also help the environment.

We also want people to realize that there are several ways to help others in need. We want children, teen, adults, and seniors to play this game, because everybody can play, donate, and spread awareness to the causes in our game.

We have highlighted the 5 main themes of charity: Animal Welfare, Education Reform, Environmental Needs, Health, and Hunger. Scroll down or click on the themes in the nav bar to explore more about each category and its corresponding charities.



We encourage you to donate and make a difference TODAY!

They say dogs are a man's best friend. They say pets can help owners fight depression and stress. Yet, we always undermine the value of many animal's lives. Environmental Issues

"The only way forward, if we are going to improve the quality of the environment, is to get everybody involved." - Richard Rogers

There are many issues with ethical violations when dealing with our environment. A few examples include: pollution of our oceans, clearing of forests, and urbanization of valleys are that dislocating wildlife. However, we are using this game to spread awareness about GMO (Genetically Modified Objects) products that are unsafe for one's health and environment.

Game Instructions: Use an arrow to shoot all the GMO's and try to get to the organic fruit basket without dying.

Click here to play the Environmental game!

Health

"The first wealth is health." - Ralph Waldo Emerson

36 million deaths each year are caused by non communicable diseases, such as cardiovascular disease, cancer, diabetes and chronic lung diseases. This is almost two-thirds of the estimated 56 million deaths each year worldwide. Over 7.5 million children under the age of 5 die from malnutrition and mostly preventable diseases, each year. This game will hopefully raise awareness about giving donations. Giving donations to the health field is one way to help them continue their research for a cure for the numerous amounts of incurable diseases.
